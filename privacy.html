Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

1. Data Collection

Kaymbu Photo Downloader is a browser extension that extracts and downloads photos from Kaymbu emails. The extension does not collect or transmit any personal data to any server.

The extension may store the following data locally in your browser:

  • Usage statistics (e.g., number of extractions or downloads)
  • User preferences and selected images

This data is stored only in Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device.

2. Future Data Collection (Optional Feedback)

In future updates, users may optionally submit feedback via Google Form:

  • Providing an email address or other information is entirely voluntary.
  • No other personal data will be collected.

3. Permissions

The extension uses the following permissions, with explanations:

  • downloads → To allow users to download selected photos.
  • storage → To save user preferences and usage statistics locally.
  • scripting → To extract photos from email pages.
  • tabs → To detect and interact with the Gmail tab for photo extraction.
  • Host permissions → To access content of the Kaymbu email page.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

All data remains on your local device. The extension does not share data with third parties.
